IEncryptedMessage

IEncryptedMessage: Pick<IMessage, "createdAt" | "receiverAddress" | "senderAddress" | "senderBoxPublicKey" | "messageId" | "receivedAt"> & { ciphertext: string; hash: string; nonce: string; signature: string }
  • Defined in packages/types/src/Message.ts:75

Removes the MessageBody, parent-id and references from the Message and adds four new fields: message, nonce, hash and signature.

  • message - The encrypted body of the message.
  • nonce - The encryption nonce.
  • hash - The hash of the concatenation of message + nonce + createdAt.
  • signature - The sender's signature on the hash.
